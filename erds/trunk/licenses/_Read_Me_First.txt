
ERDS - The European Resolution Discovery Service
=================================================

The European Resolution Discovery Service is created during Task 5.4 of
EuropeanaConnect(http://www.europeanaconnect.eu), a project initiated and
funded by the European Commision.

It aims to develop a solid, scalable technical infrastructure for
Europeana (http://www.europeana.eu).

Please see the homepages of both projects for more information.
